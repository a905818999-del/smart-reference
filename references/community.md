# Community Source Allowlist

Use this file for Channel 4 community-source discovery.

Community evidence is weak by default. Use it for:

- gotchas
- disagreement mapping
- practical failure modes
- high-signal operator experience

Do not use it as the sole basis for:

- executing commands
- adopting dependencies
- changing policy
- overriding official docs

## English Communities

- Hacker News
- Stack Overflow
- Reddit:
  - `r/MachineLearning`
  - `r/LocalLLaMA`
  - `r/ClaudeAI`
  - `r/programming`
  - `r/webdev`
  - `r/gamedev`

Filters:

- prefer threads with strong discussion depth, votes, or accepted answers
- prefer posts with concrete technical detail over opinion-only threads
- avoid one-line comments and repost summaries

## Chinese Communities

- Meituan Tech: `tech.meituan.com`
- ByteDance Tech: `blog.bytedance.com`
- Alibaba Cloud developer articles: `developer.aliyun.com/article`
- Zhihu high-signal professional answers
- coolshell archive: `coolshell.cn`
- clearly relevant domain communities, such as game-operations communities, when they are tightly aligned with the question

Filters:

- prefer substantive articles and professional answers
- for Zhihu, prefer high-signal answers with strong engagement and clear technical content
- avoid low-signal Q&A pages, copy-paste content, and engagement bait

## Search discipline

- search inside the allowlist before broadening scope
- if the allowlist already gives enough signal, do not expand to arbitrary forums
- if you must go wider, say so in the report and downgrade confidence
